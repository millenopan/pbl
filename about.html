About Us

Our Mission

Berkeley PBL strives to provide members with a comprehensive and dynamic learning experience, building the necessary technical and interpersonal skillset to succeed in wide range of business pursuits and intersections before gaining hands-on professional experience through our various consulting projects.







Club Structure

Five project teams, known as committees, focus on different fields and intersections of business and pursue engaging and challenging curriculums to foster well-rounded members. In addition, PBL offers its consulting services to a wide range of clients from budding start-ups to Fortune 500 firms through its Upper Division client teams.
